Cookie Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

We value your privacy

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit websites.

  • Help websites remember your preferences
  • Enable essential website functionality
  • Improve your browsing experience
  • Do not contain personally identifiable information

2. Types of Cookies We Use

We use different types of cookies:

  • Essential: Necessary for website operation
  • Performance: Help us improve our service
  • Functionality: Remember your preferences
  • Analytics: Understand how visitors use our site

3. Managing Cookies

You can control cookie settings:

  • Browser settings allow cookie management
  • Essential cookies cannot be disabled
  • Opt-out of analytics cookies below
  • Disabling cookies may affect functionality
(Required)

4. Third-Party Cookies

We may use services that set their own cookies:

  • Google Analytics for usage statistics
  • Hotjar for user experience research
  • Advertising partners (if applicable)
  • Social media platforms

5. Cookie Duration

Cookies remain on your device for different periods:

  • Session cookies: Expire when you close browser
  • Persistent cookies: Remain for set period
  • Maximum duration of 12 months
  • You can manually delete cookies anytime
