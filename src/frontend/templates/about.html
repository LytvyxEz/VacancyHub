About Vacancy Hub

Revolutionizing the way job seekers and employers connect through data-driven insights

Our Story

Founded in 2023, Vacancy Hub began as a small team of data scientists and HR professionals who recognized a fundamental problem in the job market. We saw that both job seekers and employers were making decisions without access to real-time market data, leading to inefficiencies and mismatches.

What started as a research project quickly evolved into a comprehensive platform that now serves thousands of users across multiple industries, helping them make smarter, data-driven career and hiring decisions.

Our Mission

To democratize access to job market intelligence, empowering individuals and organizations to make informed career and hiring decisions through transparent, real-time data analytics.

Our Vision

A world where everyone has access to transparent, data-driven insights about the job market, creating perfect matches between talent and opportunity on a global scale.

Our Core Values

Data-Driven

We believe in making decisions based on facts and analytics, not assumptions.

User-Centric

Our users are at the heart of everything we design and build.

Innovative

We constantly push boundaries to solve problems in new ways.

Integrity

We're committed to ethical data practices and transparency.
